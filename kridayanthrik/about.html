ABOUT OUR SYMPO

The Kridayanthrik event solely acts on the motto "LEARN ENGINEERING THROUGH SPORTS". This event is an initiative taken by our mechanical department to encourage engineering students to raise awareness regarding engineering in daily lives apart from their theoretical knowledge by inducing curiosity through involving sports in every aspect in our event, so as to make it educational as well as fun.
Kridayanthrik has become a significant milestone in our mechanical department,Last year, we were honoured by Mr. Krishnamachari Srikkanth (CHEEKA) was the Guest Of Honor, and we had the pleasure of hosting this event for around 900 attendees, who were not only captivated by the magnitude of the event but also inspired by the insights and knowledge shared.

Kridayanthrik is set to take place on 9th and 10th of November at our campus in Thandalm. The agenda will encompass engaging panel discussions, insightful presentations, and networking opportunities, all designed to foster collaboration and drive progress within the motto "Learn Engineering through sports".



GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Participants are insisted to register at the front desk and collect your access ID CARDS.
2. Participants are requested to check-in before 9.00am at the registration desk at the day of event.
3. Only registered participants will be allowed to attend the events.
4. Participants are requested to collect registration kit once the payment is over.
5. No refund on the registration fees will be encouraged.
6. Make a note of the register number which is generated when you fill the google form.
7. Payments can be done only for those who have filled the google forms.
8. Participants should wear the provided id cards at all time which will give you access to the venues inside the college.
9. Proper attire should be followed by the participants.
10. Students should refrain from abusive language, obscene display and maintain a good decorum will be appreciated.
11. Participants competing in NON-TECHNICAL group events (Chennai-28, Football and Treasure hunt) are requested to check-in with the respective help desks for registering and team head counts, FIRST COME FIRST SERVE will be followed.
12. Participants are requested to check the timing constraints and check-in before the closure of events.
13. Smoking, consumption of alcohol or any such substance is strictly prohibited inside the college premises.
14. Rules are subject to changes at the discretion of the management or the event organisers.
